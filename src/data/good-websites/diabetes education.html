DIABETES EDUCATION

Liberty Medical - an invaluable resource for diabetes education.

You can count on Liberty Medical as a source of helpful diabetes information and education. The nation's leading provider of diabetes products and supplies, Liberty® offers comprehensive diabetes education for controlling diabetes. Whether you're seeking diabetes information about a diagnosis, news about the latest diabetes treatments or education on proper food preparation, you'll find it all at LibertyMedical.com.

Learn more about diabetes education and Liberty Medical.

Find recipes, articles, video and other diabetes education materials.

As a company focused on making life easier for people living with diabetes, we know that education about diabetes and controlling diabetes is essential. At LibertyMedical.com, you'll discover a wealth of diabetes education resources and diabetes information, including:

  • Videos: our videos include helpful instructions on food preparation, and inspirational videos that can help motivate you to make the changes you need to effectively manage your diabetes.
  • Recipes: diet is critical for those living with diabetes you'll find a treasure trove of delicious and healthy recipes. From Cinnamon-Streusel Coffee Cake to Mediterranean Portobello Burgers to Sauteed Escarole with Garlic, our recipes are designed to deliver lots of flavor while helping you manage your blood glucose levels.
  • Articles: you can get an education on the basics of diabetes or research nutrition, treatments, diabetes and exercise, diabetes medication and much more.

Liberty's diabetes information and resources - including diabetes recipes, articles and more - are available through Liberty's YouTube channels, Facebook page, and Twitter page. Or visit our Ask-A-CDE page for more answers to questions about diabetes.

Liberty is a one-stop resource for diabetes products, supplies and education.

In addition to diabetes education, Liberty offers essential products and diabetic products and supplies for people living with diabetes:

  • Glucose meters. Liberty provides a wide selection of blood glucose meters to meet your health needs and lifestyle. Liberty's helpful customer care representatives are ready to help you choose the blood glucose meter that is right for you - and also ask about our free diabetes meter opportunities.
  • Insulin. Order insulin supplies from the convenience of your home. Liberty handles Medicare and insurance claim forms and reminds you when it's time to reorder.
  • Insulin pumps. Choose from pumps made by leading manufacturers such as Medtronic, Animas and ACCU-CHEK.
  • Continuous glucose monitoring systems. As the latest in diabetes testing devices, these monitors provide up to the minute results and around the clock glucose readings.
  • Other supplies, including CPAP supplies, ostomy supplies and incontinence supplies.

Liberty makes life easier by shipping right to your front door. And with free shipping** and a No Risk Guarantee* on diabetic products, it's no wonder Liberty is the choice of more than 230,000 doctors and one million people living with diabetes nationwide.
