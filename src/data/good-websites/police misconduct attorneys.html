Illinois Police Misconduct Lawyers

Chicago Attorneys Holding the Police Accountable

At The Law Offices of Neil L. Toppel & The Law Offices of Richard Dvorak in Chicago, Illinois, we do not back down from a legal battle against law enforcement. If the police engaged in brutality and misconduct that caused you to be injured, we will serve as your most diligent advocate in holding them accountable.

Police Brutality Violating Your Civil Rights

Police brutality is unacceptable under any circumstances and violates your civil rights. Injured clients come to us after being charged with a crime, but keep in mind that you don't have to be arrested to be a victim of police misconduct. Illegal searches and seizures, for example, often occur without anyone ever being arrested. Our separate law firms also handle wrongful conviction, police shootings, and wrongful death cases.

From their separate law firms in Chicago, attorneys Neil L. Toppel and Richard Dvorak provide relentless advocacy to Cook County clients who are facing criminal charges or are confronting a dispute involving a civil rights violation. Call Mr. Toppel (312-663-9500) or Mr. Dvorak (312-593-7146) today to arrange an initial consultation with an experienced attorney. Richard Dvorak is fluent in Spanish, and Neil L. Toppel has Spanish-speaking staff available.

Experienced Lawyers Handling Your Criminal Defense and Police Brutality Case

If you were arrested and victimized by police, our attorneys at The Law Offices of Neil L. Toppel & The Law Offices of Richard Dvorak are equipped to handle both your criminal defense case and your civil rights claim for police brutality or other police misconduct. First, we establish your defense against a felony or misdemeanor charge and seek the best resolution to your case. At the same time, we will investigate your claim of police brutality, false arrest, or malicious prosecution.

Detailed Investigations of Police Misconduct Involving Excessive Force

Our detail-oriented investigation in cases of police misconduct will involve thorough reviews of medical and police records prior to filing a lawsuit against the department. The experience of our attorneys in this area has resulted in millions of dollars in settlements and verdicts for our clients.
