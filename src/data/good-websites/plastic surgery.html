Plastic Surgery Richardson

There are many options in plastic surgery offered by nearby Richardson plastic surgeon Dr. Ronald Friedman to help you achieve a better overall appearance.

Why choose Dr. Friedman for all your cosmetic surgery needs?

As a gifted wood and marble sculptor, Dr. Friedman adds his artistic vision to benefit his patients.

Dr. Friedman has had his own practice since 1996, is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery (ABPS) and is a member of the well-respected American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), as well as many other medical organizations.

In addition, Dr. Friedman has been published in leading plastic surgery journals and has authored numerous textbook chapters. Our Southlake plastic surgery professional has also been featured on multiple television and radio broadcasts, and lectures both nationally and internationally.

Dr. Friedman has two offices, Flower Mound and Plano, Texas, where he offers procedures for the face, body, and breasts, including body contouring, breast surgery, and facial rejuvenation.

Some of Dr. Friedman's popular procedures are:

  • Liposuction ("lipoplasty")
  • Tummy tuck surgery ("abdominoplasty")
  • Breast augmentation surgery ("augmentation mammaplasty")
  • Facelift surgery ("rhytidectomy")
  • Eyelid surgery ("blepharoplasty")

For information on the above procedures, feel free to browse through our procedure pages, which include before and after photos.

Our plastic surgery professional near Grapevine offers a comfortable and relaxed environment to fulfill all your cosmetic surgery needs. To have your questions and/or concerns answers, feel free to contact Dr. Friedman.
