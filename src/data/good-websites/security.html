Innovative Security Company

Creative Security Company, Inc., (CSC) offers a comprehensive set of security services designed to meet the needs of a wide array of clients, ranging from individuals and small businesses with private security needs to multinational corporations and public sector organizations needing security services on a global scale.

Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, CSC is a law enforcement-based security services company owned and operated by former San Jose Police officers focused on providing clients with a new and innovative approach to security services - one that blends our law enforcement background and knowledge of leading-edge security technologies with a customizable set of cost-effective, high-quality security services.

Our comprehensive "Security-By-Design" approach enables us to tailor our security services in order to satisfy our client's security requirements, whatever they might be. By taking this approach, we can effectively reduce the total cost of security, and at the same time, minimize overall risk. With this approach, we are able to provide our clients with a substantially higher level of security service and protection than any other security company.

Innovative Security Services

Creative Security Company specializes in providing innovative and customized security services to our clients. CSC offers its clients a single source for a wide variety of security services and products.

In addition to our Security-By-Design approach, we offer clients a full suite of standalone security services, each providing cost-effective solutions to solve our client's security concerns.

Our core set of security services include Site Security & Patrols, Private & Corporate Investigations, Pre-Employment Background Screening & Background Searches and Burglar Alarms. Within these four divisions, CSC is capable of providing our clients with a total security solution tailored to the meet their specific needs, including protecting their physical and human assets.

Creative Security Company's Site Security & Patrols division includes security officers with extensive public safety training and law enforcement backgrounds, earning us a strong reputation for providing clients with the most talented and highly-skilled security staff in the industry. CSC's Backgrounds division is comprehensive and includes Pre-Employment Background Screening, Tenant Background Screening, International Screening, and Drug Screening services, among many others. CSC's Private & Corporate Investigations are conducted by expert investigators with law enforcement training and extensive investigative experience. Additionally, CSC's Burglar Alarm division provides wireless and hard-wired security systems for businesses and homes. Within this division, CSC also provides the state-of-the-art Snitch Security System to commercial real estate companies, which can secure Anything, Anywhere, Anytime for a fraction of the cost of traditional security measures.

In addition to the core security services, CSC provides additional services and security specialists in areas such as accident reconstruction, computer forensics, IT security, or, if needed, access to Special Forces members.

After initially meeting with a client, CSC can assess their security needs with a risk assessment analysis and quickly design an overall security program that's specifically tailored to address their security concerns through the most cost-effective means possible.

In short, Creative Security Company offers innovative and diverse security services to meet any security need.
