Withholding Requirements:

  • Register new businesses by filing Form REG -1(Business Registration Application)
  • Employee's Illinois Withholding Allowance: IL-W-4
  • Withholding Method = 5%
  • Supplemental Rate = Withhold at 5%

Local Taxes:

Chicago Employer Expense Tax - $4 per month per employee and commission merchant (in excess of 49)

State Unemployment Insurance (SUI):

Register as employer by filing Form UI-1 (Report to Determine Liability)
Report quarterly wages and contributions by filing Form UI-3/40 Employer's Contribution and Wage Report) by last day of month following end of quarter.

Wage base is $12,740 for 2011
Rates range from 0.550% to 9.45%
New employers use 4.350% except new construction employer which should use 5.250%.
Rates include the 0.550% fund-building factor

State Disability Insurance (SDI):

none

State labor laws:

Minimum Wage - $8.25

Termination Pay - Fired/Quit - next regular payday.
