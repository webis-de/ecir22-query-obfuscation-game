Equal Opportunity Employer

The Principal is fully committed to equal employment opportunity for all employees and applicants for employment.

The Company's Equal Employment Opportunity policy is to recruit and hire applicants for employment and train and promote employees regardless of age, race, color, religion, sex, gender identity, pregnancy, national origin, citizenship status, disability, genetic characteristics, sexual orientation, marital status, domestic partner status, or veteran status (having served on active duty during a war or in a campaign or expedition for which a campaign badge has been authorized). The Company also prohibits harassment on these bases. All such employment decisions are based on individual merit, qualifications and competence as they relate to the particular positions.
