Consumer Protection Legislation

Many different state and federal laws have been passed to help protect consumer rights. However, many of these consumer rights laws are confusing and our lawyers in Hennepin County and Ramsey County are here to explain them to you and use them to bring about justice for our clients when the laws have been violated. Some of the consumer rights laws we use to help clients uphold their consumer rights include:

  • Fair Credit and Lending Act
  • Fair Debt Collection Practices Act
  • Federal Fair Credit Reporting Act
  • Minnesota’s Lemon Law
  • Minnesota’s Consumer Fraud Act
  • Minnesota’s Deceptive Practices Act
  • Minnesota Consumer Access to Reports Act
  • Bankruptcy Abuse Prevention and Consumer Protection Act

The violation of these consumer rights laws can result in civil litigation, including a breach of contract case if a company does not honor the terms and conditions they promised when selling you a product or service. Additionally, if you have been discriminated against based on age when purchasing any kind of item, you may have a cause of action.

You may hold companies and creditors accountable for their unfair and illegal actions. Consumer protection and consumer rights are part of the checks and balances that a fair minded society relies upon to promote and maintain justice. If you feel you have been wronged, seek out qualified counsel by contacting Neff Law Firm, P.A., today to schedule an initial consultation.
