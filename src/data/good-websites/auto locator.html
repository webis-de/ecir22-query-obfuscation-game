Auto Locators

Looking for a new or pre-owned automobile?

Let your Credit Union put you in the driver’s seat the fast and easy way with Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators. Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators is an Auto Buying Service designed exclusively to assist Credit Union of Georgia members in the purchase of their next new or pre-owned vehicle.

Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators has over 50 years of automotive experience and the ability to offer the most complete range of products and services in the industry to members just like you! Their professional and courteous staff will take the time to understand your wants and needs in your next vehicle purchase.  Best of all, they eliminate the hassles and headaches of negotiating the best price with the dealership.

All you have to do is call 678-322-2063 and tell the Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators representative the make, model, color, and options you would like to see on your next vehicle and they’ll take care of the rest. Also, because they work independently from car manufacturers and dealers, there’s no pressure or obligation to purchase. This is a FREE service for Credit Union of Georgia members.

Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators is committed to providing you with:

  • Discount Pricing
  • Convenience
  • Industry Leading Products
  • Trustworthy Service

Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators’ formula for auto buying consists of:

Knowledge & Communication

  • Knowledge is power and Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators has extensive resources available to assist you in selecting your next vehicle. Their information ranges from safety and performance ratings to the latest factory rebates available. You’ll feel confident in your vehicle selection knowing that you made an educated decision.
  • Trained Representatives: From locating the car, truck, or SUV that meets your exact specifications to negotiating the best price, knowledgeable Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators representatives will do all the work for you.
  • A Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators representative will respond to you within 24-hours to any inquires or requests you may have and provide you with step-by-step updates throughout the purchase process.

Dependability

  • Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators stand by their word – they believe their reputation is everything and have the goal to never disappoint.
  • Extended Service Agreements: Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators can provide you with added protection against mechanical breakdown with extended service agreements at discounted prices. You’ll have peace of mind knowing that your vehicle is protected and your agreement is 100% transferable.

Quality & Value

  • Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators thoroughly inspects all of their vehicles prior to sale and/or resale
  • Volume discounts are passed down to you, providing you with discounted pricing on your next new or used vehicle purchase. Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators can even assist you in negotiating the highest possible trade-in amount for your current vehicle.
  • Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators locates and sells the best vehicles on the market at the best prices.
Contact our FREE Auto Buying Service today via email at autobuyingservice@CUofGA.org or by phone at 678-322-2063.

Financing Your Vehicle

Before contacting Credit Union of Georgia Auto Locators, get pre-approved for a low interest vehicle loan with Credit Union of Georgia. With an understanding of your purchasing power, you can customize your next vehicle selection to fit your budget. As a Credit Union of Georgia member, you can enjoy up to 100% financing including tax, tag and title, as well as terms from 2-6 years.

For complete details call our MAPS (Mail and Phone Services) Department at 678-486-1111 or visit any branch location or apply for a loan today with our easy online loan application.
