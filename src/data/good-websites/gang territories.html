Chicago Gang Territory vs 2011 Chicago Homicides

Posted Dec 21, 2011 at 10:27 AM
By Whet Moser

As part of David Bernstein and Noah Isackson's excellent piece on politicians and gangs in Chicago, we included a map of Chicago gang territory based on CPD information. Out of curiosity, I plotted 2011's homicides (through 12/11) and overlaid them on the gang-territory map. It's approximate, but it's a useful rough sketch of the overlap.
