Free STD Testing

Free, Anonymous HIV Testing @ the QRC (no blood or needles!)

Testing is available the first Tuesday of the month at the QRC from 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM. Testing will be available the second, third, and forth Tuesdays of the month at Health Education Outreach (HEO) from 11:00 am - 1:00 pm.

To schedule an appointment:
Call Health Education Outreach (HEO) at (909) 607.3602
FMI: jenniferb@cuc.claremont.edu
