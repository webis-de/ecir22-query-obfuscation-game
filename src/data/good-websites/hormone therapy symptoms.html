What is Natural Hormone Therapy?

Bioidentical Hormone Therapy is known by a variety of different names – hormone replacement therapy, natural hormone therapy, HRT, etc. The reason it is often referred to as Natural Hormone Therapy is because the hormone we prescribe, whether HGH – Human Growth Hormone – or testosterone, is an exact replica of the hormone made by your body. Therefore, the body accepts it as its own naturally-produced hormone. Hence, the name Natural Hormone Therapy.
