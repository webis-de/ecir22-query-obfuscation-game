Welcome to the rebellion!
From now on, you are part of our resistance movement against the dystopia we are currently living in.
As you know, a few years ago the government passed a law that changed our time radically. The law abolished data protection completely, and our nation became a police state. They even keep a watch on every search query people submit to search engines. To prevent this from going on, our resistance group is secretly helping web users to obfuscate their private search queries, rendering the surveillance of the government useless. We will need all the help and creativity we can get.
Let the rebellion start!
